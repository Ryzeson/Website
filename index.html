How to play
How to play
Rules:
The object of the game is to collect all of the flags in each level, and then make it back to the portal to advance to the next level before time runs out. Colliding with enemies will subtract time. The levels get tougher the deeper you go...

Controls:
Use the arrow keys to move the player.
Once the compass is collected, hold "o" to zoom out. (You can't move while zoomed out.)
